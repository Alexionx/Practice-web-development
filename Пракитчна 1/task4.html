Marzipan reciep

Ingredients

  • 250 g nuts
  • 1 spon of sugar
  • 40ml apple juice

Instructions

  1. Put 250 g of walnuts on a dry baking sheet and dry them in the oven at a temperature of 120 degrees for 15 minutes.
  2. Bring 40 ml of apple juice to a boil.
  3. In the bowl of the blender, in small portions, add 250 g of dried nuts and grind them into fine crumbs.
  4. Transfer the chopped nuts to a wide bowl, add 1 tbsp. l. sugar, 40 ml of hot juice and mix thoroughly until combined into a homogeneous mass.
  5. Wrap the resulting mass in cling film and put it in the refrigerator for 1-2 hours.
  6. After 1-2 hours, remove the nut-sugar mass from the refrigerator, remove the cling film. Form a long sausage with your hands. Cut the sausage with a knife into slices about 1.5 cm thick, and shape each such slice into a neat little ball.
